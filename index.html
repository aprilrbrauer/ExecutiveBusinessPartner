About Me

I'm an Executive Assistant Business Partner with more than two decades of experience supporting some of the most demanding leaders in tech and government — from a Board-appointed Port Auditor to VPs at Intuit overseeing 60+ person organizations.

My edge isn't just logistics — it's systems thinking. I design the structures that keep an executive's day from becoming reactive: color-coded calendar architectures, AI-powered briefing tools, communication frameworks, and event machines that actually run themselves.

Recently, I've gone deep on AI workflow design — building custom automations with Claude and Cowork that eliminate hours of manual prep work per week. I don't just use AI tools; I build them, tailored for the specific operating rhythms of senior executives.

I'm based in San Diego and open to remote and hybrid opportunities across tech and business.
